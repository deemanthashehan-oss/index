📖 Basic Laws of Logarithms

log₁₀(ab) = log₁₀a + log₁₀b Product Law
log₁₀(a/b) = log₁₀a - log₁₀b Quotient Law
log₁₀(aⁿ) = n × log₁₀a Power Law
log₁₀(1) = 0 Zero Law
log₁₀(10) = 1 Base Law
log₁₀(10ⁿ) = n Exponent Law
💡 Example: log₁₀(100) = log₁₀(10²) = 2
💡 Example: log₁₀(2×3) = log₁₀2 + log₁₀3

📝 Worked Examples

Example 1: log₁₀(8) + log₁₀(125) = log₁₀(8×125) = log₁₀(1000) = 3
Example 2: log₁₀(1000) - log₁₀(10) = log₁₀(1000/10) = log₁₀(100) = 2
Example 3: log₁₀(2³) = 3 × log₁₀(2)
Example 4: log₁₀(5) + log₁₀(20) = log₁₀(5×20) = log₁₀(100) = 2
